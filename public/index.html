NKL Duel League

The NA-based dueling league for Mordhau players looking to test their 1-versus-1 mettle in an organized setting! We have over 170 players of varying skill levels signed up, so if you want to improve your dueling skills or just want to get a taste for some organized 1-on-1 competition, you've come to the right place!

NKL Events

The National Knights League has big plans,
so stay tuned on our Discord server for updates.

Live Events

We stream all of our competitions, breakdowns, and discussions on Twitch. We also upload matches to our YouTube channel! Whenever we go live, you'll be notified in our Discord server.

Duels

We host servers across North America, so anyone can come and compete! We even have our own public duelyard for players to practice whenever they want.

Cash Prizes

The best-performing players can win some serious cash. Currently, the Season 1 prize pool is at 1200 USD!

Crown Prize!

With over 170 contestants signed up, a Crowned Barbute cosmetic will also be rewarded to the Season 1 Duel League champion!

All Star Weekend

A draft 2v2 and 3v3 tourney where we celebrate the halfway point of the season. Everyone is welcome to join this event to get in on some team fight exhibition action!
